Services, 2005), a discontinued Windows application. Specifically, it comprises
**body measurements** and **garment construction constraints** — that is, facts:
numeric sizing tables and geometric/physical rules about how knitted garments go
together. Facts are not copyrightable.

No code, pattern prose, or other authored text from the original application is
used in this project. The instruction-generating sentences, the manuals, and the
program source are **not** reproduced here. Our own encoding of the constraint
rules (`data/constraints.json`) and our own written patterns are original work.
